Historic Places In Cape Town

District Six Museum

  • This museam gives a remembrance to the events of the apartheid era
  • The ground floor is covered by a large street map of District Six.
  • Its function as a museum serves as a memorial to the community

Castle Of Good Hope

  • The Castle is the oldest existing building in South Africa.
  • The fortress housed a church, bakery, various workshops,
    living quarters, shops, and cells, among other facilities.
  • The Castle acted as local headquarters for the South African Army in the Western Cape

Iziko Slave Lodge

  • The Slave Lodge is a South African social history museum located in Cape Town.
  • The Slave Lodge is one of the oldest buildings in Cape Town and
    was previously known as the South African Cultural History Museum.
  • The lower level of this museum explores the long history of slavery in South Africa.
